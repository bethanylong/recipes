World Serpent Red IPA
=====================

10 gallon all-grain

Malts
-----
* 21# Pale 2-row
* 3# Munich 10L
* 1# Special B
* 2oz black barley

Boil
----
* 2.5oz Sorachi Ace 90min
* 1.5# brown sugar 30min
* 2oz Citra 2min
* 2.5oz Citra 0min (end of boil)

Dry hop: 1oz Citra after fermentation

Yeast: American Ale
